class: shoe
attributes: shoeSize (integer), shoeColor (string),
hasLaces (boolean), shoeVersion(float), dateCreated (datetime)
methods:
tie_shoe - ties the shoe if the hasLaces attribute is true.
place_on_foot - places shoe on foot if the shoeSize value
is greater than the footSize.
create_shoe - the manufacture creates the show which
gives a value to the dateCreated attribute.
