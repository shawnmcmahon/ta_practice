instance: bicycle

attributes: numberOfTires (integer), brakeType (string),
isFullOfAir (boolean)

refill_tire: fills the tire full of air if the IsFullOfAir
is value is false.
brake_back_tire: Uses the handbrake to come to a stop if the
brakeType is the correct value.
replace_tires: Replaces the tires of the bike depending on the
value of numberOfTires.
